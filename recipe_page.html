How to Make Pancakes

Steps to Make Pancakes

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, eggs, and melted butter until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup, fruits, or toppings of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
